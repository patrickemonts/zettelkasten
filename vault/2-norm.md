#norms #matrix

The 2-normof a $m \times n$ matrix is defined as follows
$$
\Vert M \Vert_2 = \sqrt{\lambda_{\rm max}(M^\dagger \ M)} = \sigma_{\rm max}(M)
$$
where $$ \sigma(M)$$ are singular values.

The 2-norm is smaller than the [[Frobenius norm]]

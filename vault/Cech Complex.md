 >[! definition]
 > Let $X$ be a collection of points in $\mathbb{R}^n$. For each $x \in X$, let $B_\epsilon(x)$ be a closed ball centered at $x$ with radius $\epsilon$. The ÄŒech complex of $X$ and $\epsilon$ is the [[Nerve|nerve]] of  $$
\mathcal{C}_\epsilon(X)=\{S \subset X: \cap_{x\in S} B_\epsilon(x)\neq \emptyset\}
$$

The computational basis is the eigenbasis of the $\sigma_z$, the Pauli $Z$ operator. The basis states are 
    $$ \ket{0}=\begin{pmatrix}1\\0\end{pmatrix},\quad \ket{1}=\begin{pmatrix}0\\1\end{pmatrix}.$$
